Privacy Policy for Istafsar

Effective Date: August 1, 2025

1. About Istafsar

Istafsar is an Islamic Question & Answer app where users can ask religious questions. Only approved and verified scholars may answer these questions. The app fosters religious learning and guidance within an authenticated environment.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Location (if granted)
  • Profile Photo

We also collect user-submitted content such as questions and comments. We do not collect sensitive data like health or biometric information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your profile alongside submitted questions/comments
  • Allow scholars to respond to questions
  • Optionally match users with local scholars based on location
  • Maintain app functionality via Supabase services

We do not use your data for advertising or behavior tracking.

4. Authentication and Storage

We use Supabase Auth for email/password login. All data is securely stored using Supabase. Social login options may be added in future updates.

5. Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera – for uploading profile pictures
  • Location – for region-based features (optional)

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. All data stays within our app and Supabase services.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. If discovered, such data will be promptly deleted.

8. Data Access and Deletion

Currently, account deletion is not available within the app. Users can request account or data deletion by contacting us at:

9. Security Measures

We implement standard security practices to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top. Please review this policy occasionally.
